REZZ

Biography

REZZ (Isabelle Rezazadeh) is a Canadian electronic music producer born in Ukraine and raised in Canada.

Her music is defined by deep bass, slow tempos and industrial textures, creating a hypnotic, trance-like atmosphere.

Today, REZZ is recognized as one of the most distinctive artists in modern electronic music.
